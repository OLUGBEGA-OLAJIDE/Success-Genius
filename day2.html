POLYGON

The word 'poly' means many and the word 'gon' means angled. Therefore, a polygon means a many angled. In mathematics,we have the word polygon to be a many angled shape.

Therefore the general name for a 2-dimensional shape is polygon. The simplest form of a polygon is a triangle. All quadrilaterals are also examples of polygon. However because triangles and quadrilaterals have their own special properties they are usually called specially. and Generally, polygon derive their names from the number of sides and the types of angles included. TRIANGLES

A Triangle is a three sided polygon(plane figure) with three angles.

Types of Triangles

(a) A scalene triangle has no side and no angle equal.

(b) An equilateral triangle has all sides and angles equal.

(c) An isosceles triangle has just two sides and angles equal.

SQUARES

Squares are quadrilaterals which has all sides equal. Squares are special quadrilaterals and even special shape because there is no other quadrilateral which has all sides equal.
