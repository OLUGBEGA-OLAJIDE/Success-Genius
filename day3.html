In your past classes, you must have come across the meaning and properties of a circle. Circle is a plane shape i.e a 2D(Two dimensional shape). The parts of a circle include; segment, chord, arc, sector, circumference, radius or radii, diameter and Annulus.

In words, the formula for finding the:

area of the a sector=x*3.14*radius*radius/360

area of a cicle=pie(3.14)*radius*radius

length of an arc=x*3.14*2*radius/360

Area of annulus=3.14(R*R-r*r

Where we have the x to be in degree
